Virtual Appointments

To set up an appointment, please use our Outlook calendar / appointment system to sign up in advance for a 30-minute window.

Select the person you would like to meet with (descriptions of the areas of expertise for each person appear below their name), and then select the date for your appointment in the calendar (dates that the selected person holds their office hours are highlighted in bold). Now select the 30-minute slot you would like to the right of the calendar (if no slots appear, then that person is already fully booked on that date).

You will receive an email confirming your booking, along with a Zoom link (on Tuesdays, the password for the Zoom session is ‘trex’).
